*** classical understanding
- components of angular momentum (3 in cartesian plane) as cross product:
    $$L = r \times p =  \begin{bmatrix}
                        x & y & z
                        \end{bmatrix} \times \begin{bmatrix}
                        p_x & p_y & p_z
                        \end{bmatrix}
                        = \begin{bmatrix}
                        i & j & k\\
                        x & y & z\\
                        p_x & p_y & p_z
                        \end{bmatrix}$$
$$L = i(yp_z - p_yz) - j(xp_z - p_xz) + k(xp_y - p_xy) \\
    = iL_x - jL_y + kL_z$$
